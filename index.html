Privacy Policy for Decrypt

Last Updated: 09/04/2025

Introduction

Welcome to Decrypt! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application, Decrypt.

We take your privacy seriously and have designed Decrypt to minimize data collection. This game is primarily designed to function offline, without requiring or collecting personal information.

Information We Don't Collect

Decrypt is designed with your privacy in mind. We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • Personal identifiers such as name, email address, or phone number
  • Login credentials or account information
  • Location data
  • Browsing history
  • Device information beyond what is provided by the operating system for basic functionality
  • Photos, contacts, or other content from your device

Information We Do Collect

App Usage Data

The App stores your game progress, settings, and achievements locally on your device only. This includes:

  • Game progress (completed puzzles and categories)
  • In-game achievements
  • Preferences and settings
  • User-selected theme and customization options

This information is stored locally on your device using the device's storage and is not transmitted to any servers.

Crash Reports

If the App crashes, anonymous crash reports may be sent to help us improve the App's stability. These reports contain technical information about the nature of the crash but do not contain personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

App Store

When you download Decrypt from the Google Play Store, Google may collect information as described in their privacy policy. We do not have access to this information. Please review Google's privacy policy for more information.

In-App Purchases

If you make in-app purchases for premium themes or developer support, the transaction is processed by the Google Play Store. We do not receive or store your payment information. Please review Google's payment policies for more information.

Children's Privacy

Our App is suitable for all ages and does not collect personal information from children or anyone else.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

By using Decrypt, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.
